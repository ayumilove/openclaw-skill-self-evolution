# Self-evolution rules (reference)

## What counts as a good learning

A learning is worth persisting when it is:
- **Repeatable**: likely to happen again
- **Actionable**: changes what to do next time
- **Specific**: includes a trigger and a response

Bad: “Be more careful.”
Good: “If `channels status` times out, re-run with `--timeout 60000` and `--probe`.”

## Where to store it

- Put **temporary context** in `memory/YYYY-MM-DD.md`.
- Put **stable preferences/rules** in `MEMORY.md`.
- Put **procedures** in a skill.
